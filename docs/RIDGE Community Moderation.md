# RIDGE Community Moderation Policy

RIDGE includes community features such as Tailgate and Trophy Room.

These features allow hunters to share photos, stories, and harvest achievements.

This document defines how community content is moderated to maintain a respectful and ethical hunting environment.

---

# 1. Community Principles

RIDGE promotes:

• ethical hunting
• respect for wildlife
• respect for other hunters
• responsible outdoor behavior

Community features should reflect the culture of responsible hunting.

---

# 2. Allowed Content

Users may post:

• harvest photos
• hunt stories
• scouting discoveries
• trail camera images
• hunting gear setups

Posts should focus on legitimate hunting experiences.

---

# 3. Prohibited Content

The following content is not allowed:

• illegal hunting activity
• poaching
• harassment or threats
• graphic or disturbing images
• explicit content
• spam or advertising without permission

Posts violating these rules may be removed.

---

# 4. Harvest Photo Guidelines

Harvest photos should follow ethical presentation guidelines.

Encouraged practices:

• respectful handling of animals
• clean presentation
• appropriate field photos

Discouraged practices:

• graphic images
• disrespectful poses
• unsafe firearm handling

---

# 5. Trophy Room Integrity

The Trophy Room showcases top harvests.

To maintain credibility:

• users must provide accurate information
• moderators may review submissions
• suspicious entries may be removed

Verification levels may include:

Unverified  
Community Verified  
Moderator Verified

---

# 6. Reporting System

Users should be able to report content that violates rules.

Reports may include:

• inappropriate images
• harassment
• illegal activity
• misinformation

Reported posts should enter a moderation queue.

---

# 7. Moderator Tools

Moderators must have tools to:

• remove posts
• review reported content
• suspend accounts
• verify Trophy Room entries

Moderation actions should be logged.

---

# 8. Account Enforcement

If users repeatedly violate community guidelines, actions may include:

• warning notifications
• temporary suspension
• permanent account removal

These measures help protect the community.

---

# 9. Fair Use of Location Information

Users should not attempt to expose another hunter's private hunting locations.

Doxxing or revealing private locations is prohibited.

---

# 10. Community Culture

RIDGE should foster a culture of:

• learning
• mentorship
• respectful competition

The Tailgate feed and Trophy Room should celebrate hunting achievements responsibly.

---

# 11. Moderation Responsibility

Moderators must enforce these guidelines consistently.

Community trust depends on fair and transparent moderation.
